The challenge for school staffing agencies

The right school‑side decision‑maker is hard to find at scale.

  • Thousands of schools, each different. For example, ≈3,250 schools in London alone and ~2,700 state schools across the UK regions you care about.
  • Contacts are scattered everywhere. Websites, staff directories, circulars, SEND reports, safeguarding policies and other PDFs.
  • Manual research is slow and inconsistent. Each recruiter builds their own ad‑hoc list, with mixed quality and lots of duplication.
  • Generic email tools give volume, not relevance. They surface emails, but rarely the actual school‑side hiring decision‑maker for your roles.

What Grappler does

End‑to‑end pipeline from school websites to a hiring‑ready CSV.

Input

TXT file of school websites.
For example: one domain per line, focused on the regions or boards you care about.

Engine

  • Crawls school pages on each domain.
  • Follows links into PDFs (policies, SEND reports, bulletins, etc.).
  • Extracts emails and phone numbers.
  • Analyses context (keywords, URLs, doc type).
  • Assigns a Score + HiringSignal to each contact.

Output

Clean CSV that drops straight into your spreadsheet/CRM workflow.

Website, Email, Phone, SourceURL, SourceType, Score,
HiringSignal, IsFreeProvider, Context

Feature deep dive 1

Website + PDF extraction that is actually schools‑aware.

Crawls

  • Public pages only — no logins, CAPTCHAs or grey‑area access.
  • Detects and downloads PDFs such as safeguarding policies, careers policies, SEND reports, bulletins and curriculum booklets.
  • Respects your target list: only the school domains you provide are touched.

Extracts

  • Emails found in HTML plus emails and phone numbers found in PDF text.
  • Captures SourceURL and SourceType (html/pdf) for every contact.
  • Optionally captures short context snippets around each email so your team can verify meaning quickly.

Feature deep dive 2

Hiring / vacancy‑aware ranking, not just a flat list.

Signals used

  • Keywords near the email: hiring, vacancy, vacancies, recruitment, careers, jobs, placement, etc.
  • URL hints: paths like /jobs, /vacancies, /careers, /recruitment.
  • Email patterns: careers@, recruitment@, hr@ vs info@.

Output fields

Score — numeric score combining signals.
HiringSignal — TRUE/FALSE flag for likely hiring relevance.

Simple example row set

info@school.org

Generic inbox on contact page.

Score 0

HiringSignal FALSE

careers@school.org

Found on /careers-and-destinations-policy.pdf.

Score 3

HiringSignal TRUE
